INSTRUCTORS & SKILL TRACKS

STORY STRUCTURE
WITH BRIAN MCDONALD

Brian McDonald leads this two-day workshop that focuses on three-act structure and theme - and how utilizing these ancient tools of storytelling can help writers create more meaningful and compelling tales. Participants will learn how to construct acts that build upon each other and propel the story forward seamlessly. You will also learn how plot and character work in concert to support a story’s theme.

Workshop materials provided.

RESERVE YOUR SEAT Brian is anauthor, chief storyteller and awards-winning screenwriter. Learn more ->
LOGOTYPE DESIGN
WITH MACKEY SATURDAY

Join Mackey Saturday in this two-day workshop for an in-depth look at designing an impactful logotype for brands of any scale. Participants will learn the necessary skills to create a distinctive and enduring logotype and come away with a better understanding of how and when different classes of typography should be used, the benefits of each individual class, and how to align brand strategy with a type style.

IMPORTANT: Participants will need Adobe Illustrator or another vector graphics program and bring their own computer with this program installed.

RESERVE YOUR SEAT Mackey is an identity designer who has worked with companies such as Instagram, Oculus and Unsplash. Learn more ->
UNCONVENTIONAL COMPOSITION
WITH ALANA LOUISE

Alana Louise leads this two-day workshop focused on her creative process of combining typography and illustration in a layout that breaks the grid. Focusing on a matchbook cover as the subject, Alana will share her process from sketching all the way to preparing a file for production. You’ll leave this class with knowledge that can be applied to projects in the real world.

IMPORTANT: Participants will need experience using Adobe Illustrator and bring their own computer with this program installed.

RESERVE YOUR SEAT Alana is an award winning commercial artist living in Austin, Texas with a passion for the outdoors. Learn more ->
PRODUCT DESIGN
WITH ROGIE KING

Join Rogie King in this two-day workshop for an in-depth look at designing and prototyping modern mobile and web applications using the new collaborative design tool, Figma. Participants will design their own application while learning how to use styles, components, animations, shared team libraries, commenting, versioning and more. You will also prototype and share your application to present user flows and animations and interactions of your application to the entire class.

IMPORTANT: Participants will need to bring their own computer.

RESERVE YOUR SEAT Rogie is the Lead Designer at Dribbble and is a veteran in the product design space. Learn more ->
VECTOR ILLUSTRATION
WITH DKNG STUDIOS

In this two-day workshop, Nathan Goldman and Dan Kuhlken lead a deep-dive into vector illustration and DKNG's creative process, using poster design as the subject. After concept development and sketching, participants will jump into Adobe Illustrator to begin building the vector structure of the poster. DKNG will also share their process of adding detail, texture, and halftones, and conclude with a show & tell of student projects, as well as a Q&A including real time examples from DKNG’s body of work.

IMPORTANT: Participants will need to be proficient in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop and bring their own computer with these programs installed.

RESERVE YOUR SEAT DKNG are master illustratore who have worked with companies such as DC Comics, Sony, and Disney. Learn more ->
BUSINESS OF CREATIVE
WITH HOODZPAH

Back by popular demand! Join Amy and Jennifer Hood in this two-day workshop focused on helping you start your own creative business, grow your client base and promote yourself without selling out or starving. You’ll get a transparent look at how to create a business plan, budget, price jobs, and communicate your value and process to your clients. You’ll learn about the tools they use day-to-day at Hoodzpah, including project management, bookkeeping, contracts, collaboration, and learn tips for monitoring your financial health.

Workshop materials provided.

RESERVE YOUR SEAT Hoodzpah is a nimble branding and design studio headquartered in Newport Beach, CA. Learn more ->

FAQ

Is Skill Camp just a more expensive conference with a smaller lineup?⁣⁣

No—Skill Camp 2020 is (6) 2-day, 16 hour workshops led by one of the top creatives in the industry. Choose one of the six skill tracks – Story Structure with Brian McDonald, Logotype Design with Mackey Saturday, Unconventional Composition with Alana Louise, Product Design with Rogie King, Vector Illustration with DKNG Studios, or The Business of Creative with Hoodzpah Design Co.. ⁣⁣We’ll end our days with inspiring talks from 2 keynote speakers.

Why Skill Camp?⁣⁣

We designed this event to supplement the lack of solid opportunities for continuing education, skill building and community outside of college and the 4-hour workshop. ⁣⁣

I’m not local to Memphis. Where should I stay?

Our friends at the Courtyard Memphis Downtown have provided Skill Camp attendees with a solid group rate. Book your room today.

Is Skill Camp for beginners?⁣⁣

No. This event is for creatives of all ages who are serious about their craft and want to sharpen their skills, learn how others work and develop in-person relationships with a larger community of creators from all over the country. Track requirements are posted above.

What will I get out of it?⁣⁣

Transformative skill growth and community. Learn skills that take your work to the next level and develop relationships and accountability with a small group of likeminded professionals. ⁣⁣
